Ingredients to Make Memaw's Cast Iron Skillet Cookie

This list of ingredients includes everything you'll need to make the cookie dough using this recipe.

  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup packed brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • 3/4 cup all purpose flour fluffed and levelled
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup chocolate chips or chunks
