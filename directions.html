Directions to Make Memaw's Cast Iron Skillet Cookie

Navigation

Home | Ingredients

  1. Make a basic chocolate chip cookie dough. You can use this recipe, or you can use store-bought cookie dough.
  2. (Optional) Add your favorite mix-ins to the cookie dough. I like to add M&M's or bits of Butterfinger candy.
  3. Lightly grease your cast iron skillet and spread the cookie dough evenly inside it.. Feel free to use whatever size skillet you'd like for the amount of cookie dough you have. I use a 6 to 8-inch skillet.
  4. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 minutes, or until the edges begin to look golden and crispy. The center should still be soft.
  5. Remove skillet from the oevn and allow it to cool for 10-20 minutes. Keep in mind that the cast iron will retain heat and keep the cookie warm for a while.
  6. Enjoy! The cookie is even better served hot with a scoop of cold ice cream on top.
